The Hanoi Method

A writing practice system built around process, repetition, and release.

  • Focus on the process.
  • Do the work.
  • Don't look back.

Write → Revise → Next.

What it is

The Hanoi Method is a way of practicing writing. Not publishing. Not performing. Practicing.

Write something short. Revise it once. Move on.

The goal is not to preserve the work. The goal is to build the skill.

What it is not

It is not a promise of inspiration. It is not a search for perfect pages. It is not writing for posterity.

It is practice. Private, disposable, repeatable practice.
